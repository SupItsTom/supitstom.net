## Scope
This program only applies to select products/services.

#### The components in-scope are:
- **[Serenity Launcher for Windows](/serenity)**
- **[Teapot Live Discord Bot](/teapot/bot)**
- **The following websites:**
  - **https://supitstom.net** and sub-domains
  

#### What im looking for:
- Malicious XSS attacks
- First-party, unmodified, software exploits
- OAuth manipulation
- Denial of Service/DDoS attacks that causes spikes or slows down the network

---

## Out of scope
Reports that are submitted under the following are not accepted:
- Self XSS or XSS that affects only out-of-date browsers
- Attacks that require physical access to the user's device
- Spamming like a retard (nmap scans are allowed but will likely get blocked by firewall)
