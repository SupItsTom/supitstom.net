## Setting your plugins
To use Teapot, it has to be loaded as a plugin.
1. Start the console.
2. Fire up **DashLaunch**.
    - If you have already setup **Xbox 360 Neighborhood**, launch it from there, otherwise use **XeXMenu**.
3. Scroll down and click on **Plugins**.
4. Click on **Plugin 2** and find `ðŸ“„Teapot.xex` and select it.
    - **Plugin1** should be set to `ðŸ“„xbdm.xex`
    - **Plugin3** is optional, `ðŸ“„JRPC2.xex` is recommended
5. Open up the **Save / Load** menu by pressing **RB**.
6. Scroll down and press **X** on **Hdd**.
7. Reboot your console.

---

## Getting Online
If successful, Teapot will bootup and ask you to register your console.
1. Hit the **Guide** button on your controller.
2. Scroll all the way to the right of the guide until you're at the **Teapot Menu**.
3. Select **Continue Now**.
4. Then enter your email, and username.

You can use your **Email** and **CPU Key** to manage your preferences, tokens, and customize your dash in the **[console manager](https://teapotlive.us/signin)**.
