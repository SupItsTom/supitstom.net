## Installation
The bot can be installed in two ways: user-installable and server-installable.

{/* :::danger[User-Installable Apps Preview]
User-installable apps are currently in preview.\
During the preview, there are major limitations and known bugs, and details are subject to change.
::: */}

### User Install
As of March 18, 2024, Discord has given us the ability to make bots user-installable.\
User-installed apps can be used across all of your servers, within (G)DMs, and in DMs with the bot user.
1. **[Click here](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1040809994012069919)** and click **Try It Now**.
2. Click on **Authorize**.

### Server Install
1. **[Click here](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1040809994012069919)** and click **Add to Server**.
2. Pick a server which you have **Manage Server** permission in.
3. Click on **Authorize**.
